Our Story

MOUN was born from a simple observation: Rwanda's growing middle class deserves beautiful, personalized living spaces — but most people either guess their way through décor decisions or rely on expensive designers with no way to preview the results.

Founded in 2025 by a group of young creatives and software engineers at ALU, MOUN combines interior design expertise with 3D digital visualization technology. We let you see your space before you spend — reducing costly mistakes and bringing your vision to life with confidence.

Our name, MOUN, reflects our philosophy: a home should feel like you. Personal, intentional, and rooted in your identity.

Our Process

A simple workflow designed to bring your vision to life — with clarity and confidence at every step.

01

Consultation

We start with a conversation — in person or on WhatsApp — to understand your space, your taste, and your budget. No pressure, no commitment required.

02

Space Assessment

You send us photos and measurements of your room. We review everything and put together a design brief that captures exactly what you are looking for.

03

3D Visualization

Our team builds a photorealistic 3D render of your space using professional design tools. You see exactly how your room will look — before anything is purchased or changed.

04

Review & Revisions

You review the 3D design and request any changes — different colours, furniture layout, décor pieces. We refine until the design feels exactly right.

05

Shop & Transform

Once the design is approved, order the exact décor pieces from our shop. We deliver to your door across Kigali and your space transforms exactly as visualized.
